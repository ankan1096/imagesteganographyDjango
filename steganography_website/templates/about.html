What Is Steganography?

Art and science of writing hidden messages in such a way that no one apart from the intended recipient knows of the existence of the message

Evolution Of Steganography

Traces of steganography existed in ancient Greece,when Herodotus narrated examples in his Stories.The first recorded use of the term was in 1499

Types Of Steganography

Steganography can be of 6 types based on the cover medium and they are known as Text, Image, Audio, Video, Network, Email Steganography

Know More
Image Steganography

Image Steganography refers to the process of hiding data within an image file.The image selected for this purpose is called the cover image and the image obtained after steganography is called the stego image

Know More
Layers Of An Image

Image steganography research usually involves grey scale images as collector image.In Grey scale image there is only one image layer.A RGB color image has three color layers i.e. Red , Green and Blue

Aim Of Our Project
  • We are decomposing an RGB cover image into itâ€™s respective three layers first. (i.e. Red Layer , Green Layer , Blue Layer)
  • Now if we use one of the cover image layers to embed our secret message using least significant bit insertion technique, then also only one third of the container capacity is filled
  • Hence it is observed that, the capacity of storing the secret information can be increased
  • According to the characteristics of human vision, the sensitivity of three components of the color image is different. It is most sensitive to green, followed by red and least sensitive to blue
  • Hence we try to embed the secret information in Blue layer followed by Red layer as much as possible and try to embed the secret information in Green layer as less as possible
